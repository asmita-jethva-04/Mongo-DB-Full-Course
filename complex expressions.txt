Complex Expressions :- 
->Complex expressions are advanced logical conditions inside a query where you combine multiple operators
-> The $expr operator allows using arggregation expressions within a query.
-> Useful when you need to compare fields from the same document in a more complex manner.

sy :- {$expr : {operator : [field , value]}}

ex :- db.products.find({$expr : {$gt : ['price' , 19.99]}})
